Our Story

How We Met

I met Eric at a mutual friend’s going away party over five years ago (thanks Drea!). As the night was winding down, a few of my friends suggested to drive out to Golden Gate Park to catch the sunrise. Me, Eric, and 5 other adventurous souls ended up cramming into a five person car to chase the sunrise. That’s when Eric and I started talking. We weren’t able to catch the sunrise (when we arrived at the park, I checked my phone and discovered the sun wasn’t going to rise for another three hours, so the mission was a failure), but we were able to catch each other’s hearts :) (Yes, cheesy, I know haha). From there, Eric messaged me the very next day on Facebook, asking me if I was the one who mentioned participating in Tough Mudder later that month, since he was also going to be doing Tough Mudder. I responded back yes, the conversation kept flowing, and it’s all history from there.

The Proposal

Karen was pretty sure a proposal was coming but I refused to give any details. On a long weekend in 2017, Karen, a group of friends, and I went to Paso Robles. Karen grew convinced that it wasn't her weekend after we had visited a few wineries and there was no proposal. On the way back to San Francisco, a small group decided to hike in Pinnacles National Park, where I knew I would spring the trap (I mean pop the question). Despite me refusing to let her anywhere near my backpack and forcing her to stand on a ledge "for photos" while the group watched, she claims she had no idea. She said yes, making me a very lucky guy!
