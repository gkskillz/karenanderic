Accommodations

If you plan on staying overnight before or after the wedding, we recommend either a hotel in Healdsburg or Windsor, or a house rental through sites such as Airbnb or VRBO. A few hotels we recommend are:

  • Hampton Inn & Suites Windsor
  • Holiday Inn Windsor Wine Country
  • Holiday Inn Express Windsor Sonoma Wine Country
  • Hotel Healdsburg

Wedding Day Transportation

Trentadue has plenty of parking, so driving to the venue should not be a problem. The winery is located approximately 10 minutes north of Healdsburg, 15 minutes north of Windsor, and 25 minutes north of Santa Rosa.

If you prefer not to drive, we are planning on providing a shuttle from Windsor and Healdsburg which will pick up before the ceremony and return after the reception. The exact details are still to be determined, but please let us know in the RSVP so we can gauge interest. Ride sharing options such as Lyft and Uber are also available in the area.

Flying In

The closest airports are all located in the San Francisco Bay Area. Both San Francisco International Airport (SFO) and Oakland International Airport (OAK) are approximately an hour and a half drive from the Geyserville / Healdsburg area. San Jose International Airport (SJC) is located to the south and is approximately a two hour drive.
