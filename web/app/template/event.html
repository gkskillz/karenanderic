Tea Ceremony

In a traditional Chinese wedding, the tea ceremony is one of the most significant events. It includes formal introductions of the bride and groom and shows respect to their families. Historically, after the bride and groom exchanged vows, the couple would serve tea to the groom's family (the bride would have served tea privately to her own family that morning). Today, many couples choose to honor both the bride and groom's families by hosting tea ceremonies for both sets of parents.

At our wedding, we will be having an informal tea ceremony for both of our families. We will start with serving Eric’s side of the family first, and then proceed with serving Karen’s side of the family. The tea ceremony will be held at Hotel Healdsburg on Saturday, August 4th, 2018 at 12pm. The whole ceremony should take approximately 30 to 45 minutes.
